About Us





FitnessCubed is creating connected fitness products to seamlessly integrate basic exercise into your daily life. In our daily lives, we often miss out on the thing that matters most to stay healthy– exercise. We find ways to make exercise accessible so you can spend time on what truly matters. Our first product is the Cubii, an under-desk elliptical trainer that lets office dwellers get some basic exercise at their desk.
